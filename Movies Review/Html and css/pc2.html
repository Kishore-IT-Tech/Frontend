Grand Theft Auto V

Discription:

Grand Theft Auto V (2013), developed by Rockstar Games, is an open-world action-adventure game set in the sprawling city of Los Santos and its surrounding areas. The game follows the intertwined stories of three protagonists: Michael De Santa, a retired bank robber seeking a stable life; Franklin Clinton, a young hustler looking for real opportunities; and Trevor Philips, a volatile criminal driven by chaos. Players can switch between the three characters as they engage in heists, missions, and side activities, while exploring a massive, dynamic world filled with cars, planes, weapons, and countless opportunities for chaos. Praised for its stunning graphics, immersive storytelling, and unmatched freedom of gameplay, GTA V has become one of the best-selling and most influential games of all time, continuing to thrive through its ever-expanding GTA Online multiplayer mode.
